SIBA · Airtable
Centralised Reporting & Operations Platform

Designed and built a dual-dashboard Airtable system to replace a fragmented workflow where invoices, receipts, and programme data were scattered across emails and chats. The platform streamlined business onboarding, follow-up tracking, grant activity recording, and expenditure reporting — making audits straightforward and stress-free.

SIBA · WA Dept. of Ecology
Criteria Air Pollution Reduction Programme

As Programme Manager, led a grant-funded environmental initiative in South King County focused on reducing criteria air pollutant exposure in underserved communities. Developed all research, workshop content, and community education materials. Facilitated 6 community workshops and produced a public-facing brochure and an ArcGIS Story Map.

13
SIBA · City of Seattle
Digital Presence Programme — 13 Small Business Websites

Under a City of Seattle Neighbourhood Matching Fund grant, designed and launched 13 no-code websites for small BIPOC businesses — spanning retail, childcare, restaurants, and spare parts shops. Each site was built from scratch on Wix, covering layout, branding, and content, giving owners a professional online presence for the first time.
